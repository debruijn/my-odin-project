Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Lasagna
  • Some sauce, or make it yourself

Steps

  1. Start with sauce
  2. Add a bit of spinach
  3. Add lasagna layer
  4. Add a bit of cheese
  5. Repeat the above a few times
  6. Top off with a lot of cheese
  7. Put in the oven on a sensible temperature until done
