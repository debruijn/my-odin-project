Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Frozen fruit
  • Soy milk
  • Yoghurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Oats
  • A blender

Steps

  1. Add frozen fruit first so it can defrost
  2. Peel and break apart banana, put in blender
  3. Add other items
  4. Occasionally blend to make sure the process goes smooth
  5. Finish with a good blend
